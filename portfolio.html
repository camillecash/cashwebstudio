Overview

A clear digital home for a clinical researcher.

The Jasmine Cash portfolio website was designed for a researcher whose work spans stroke recovery, neuromodulation, rehabilitation outcomes, and physical function. The site needed to feel credible, organized, and easy to browse without overwhelming visitors with dense academic information.

Cash Web Studio structured the site as a professional multi-page portfolio with dedicated areas for projects, expertise, publications, presentations, teaching, and recognition. The result gives visitors clear paths into Jasmine's work while keeping the experience polished across desktop and mobile.
